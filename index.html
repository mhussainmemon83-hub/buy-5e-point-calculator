Understanding the D&D 5e Point Buy System

The point buy system in Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition ensures fair, balanced character creation with flexibility for player creativity.

How Point Buy Works

Each ability starts at 8. You have 27 points to spend, with costs increasing for higher values. The maximum score via point buy is 15 before racial bonuses.
